SpeakSmart: An Adaptive Microphone

Taylor Grey and Erica Zhou (Team 16)

About SpeakSmart

A microphone that adapts to your volume and optimizes the sound experience for everyone.

By sensing the volume of a speaker into a microphone, SpeakSmart automatically adjusts the height and angle of the microphone on its stand to provide the best sound volume to an audience.

VIDEO

Product Overview

A microphone that senses the volume of a speaker into it and adjusts accordingly, providing the audience with the best sound experience possible without disrupting the speaker.
